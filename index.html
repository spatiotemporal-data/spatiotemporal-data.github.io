Chin Zhidao Project

 

Ch'in (Qin) Zhidao (秦直道) is a research project for smart transportation.

This project aims at providing some data-driven smart transportation solutions to real-world applications.

Research topics: intelligent transportation systems, spatiotemporal traffic data modeling, bus travel time estimation.

Goals

We are aiming at developing some accurate and efficient solutions to:

  • Missing traffic data imputation

  • High-dimensional and multidimensional forecasting

  • Spatiotemporal traffic forecasting in the presence of missing values
